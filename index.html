Biography

I am driven to deliver innovative, data based, solutions which provide impact and value. Not one to shy away from a challenge, I completed a PhD in 2017 which focused on improving flood forecasting skill at Monash University.

Outside of work you can find me training in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ). It is a martial art that is often described as human chess. I enjoy the challenges, exercise, and culture associated with BJJ.

After submitting my thesis I was fortunate to have the opportunity to work on an interdisciplinary project that delivered an Indonesian city a roadmap to transition to a water sensitive city.

Nearing the completion of this project I was asked to continue research from my PhD thesis and contribute to the delivery of guidelines to improve flood forecasting skill using remote sensing data. I gladly accepted.

I endeavour to deliver data driven solutions which are accessible to people with varied levels of data literacy.
